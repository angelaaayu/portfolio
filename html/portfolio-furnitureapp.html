Furniture eCommerce App

UX-UI Design

FurniCraft is an IOS ecommerce app crafted for emerging furniture designers, providing a platform to showcase their masterpieces while offering customers a source of inspiration. Tailored for a design-savvy audience, the app goes beyond traditional ecommerce by fostering creativity and connecting users with unique furniture pieces.

Application Used: Figma

Objective

The primary objective of FurniCraft is to empower new designers in the market, offering them a space to exhibit their creativity and connect with a broader audience. Simultaneously, the app aims to engage customers beyond mere transactions, encouraging prolonged use by integrating features that inspire and enrich the user experience. Through extensive research and iterative design, the app seeks to strike a balance between functionality and aesthetic appeal.

Project Details

The project begins with in-depth research on the target audience, focusing on understanding their behavior to brainstorm functionalities that extend beyond basic purchasing. Prototyping involves collecting feedback from peers and refining the app to ensure a seamless user experience. FurniCraft is designed not just as a marketplace but as a source of inspiration, aiming to keep users engaged and connected to the world of furniture design.
