GiftPay

Brand Design | Video Production

GiftPay is a revolutionary mobile application designed to transform the way individuals manage, trade, and maximize the value of their gift cards. With the growing popularity of gift cards as a preferred gifting option, there arises a need for a comprehensive solution to address the challenges associated with their usage.

Applications Used: Figma | Adobe After Effect | Adobe Photoshop | Adobe Illustrator | Adobe Premiere Pro | Adobe Audition

Objective

GiftPay aims to offer users a comprehensive platform for effectively managing and optimizing the value of their gift cards. Our app addresses common pain points experienced by gift card owners, including forgetting about unused cards, difficulty tracking balances, and the inconvenience of managing physical and digital gift cards cluttering up their wallets.

Project Details

  • Digital Wallet: GiftPay provides users with a digital wallet where they can securely store all their gift cards in one place. This eliminates the hassle of carrying physical cards and allows for easy access to card details anytime, anywhere.
  • Balance Tracking: Users can track the remaining balance on their gift cards in real-time. GiftPay sends notifications to remind users of expiring balances and suggests optimal ways to utilize their funds.
  • Purchase Gift Cards: The app allows users to purchase gift cards directly from participating retailers. With a wide range of retailers and brands available, users can easily find and buy gift cards for themselves or send them as gifts to others.
  • Trade Among Users: GiftPay facilitates peer-to-peer trading of gift cards, enabling users to exchange unwanted cards for ones they desire. This feature promotes flexibility and ensures that users get the most value out of their gift cards.
  • Retailer Integration: The platform integrates with various retailers to offer a diverse selection of gift cards across different categories, including retail, dining, entertainment, and more. Users can explore and choose from a wide array of options tailored to their preferences.

Delivery

Mood Board

Our aim was to evoke a sense of energy and vibrancy through the mood board, capturing the dynamic essence of the GiftPay brand.

Logo

The concept behind the logo design revolves around two cards interconnected, symbolizing the seamless management and exchange of gift cards facilitated by the app.

Color Palette

Drawing inspiration from the mood board, we opted for energetic colors, namely red and apricot, to infuse vitality and excitement into the brand identity.

Typography

To impart a playful feel, we selected the Cabin font, characterized by its rounded shapes, aligning perfectly with the brand's lively persona.

Website Home Page

Our website's home page serves as a visual representation of the brand identity, effectively communicating GiftPay's unique positioning and offerings to potential users.

Social Media Page

We curated a social media presence to extend the brand's image across various platforms, ensuring consistency and resonance with our target audience.

Pitch Video

Our ultimate deliverable was a compelling pitch video introducing the GiftPay app. Through this video, we showcased how GiftPay addresses customer pain points and highlighted its standout features, inviting viewers to experience the solution firsthand. We hope you enjoy the presentation and find value in our innovative approach.
