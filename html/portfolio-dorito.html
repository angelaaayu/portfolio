Dorito Crunch Carnival

Video Production and Editing

Dorito Crunch Carnival entails the creation of a vibrant 30-second promotional video meticulously crafted for digital platforms. This dynamic video serves a singular purpose: to radiate the exhilarating essence of a "crunch carnival."

Technologies Used: Adobe Premiere Pro | Adobe Photoshop

Objective

Engineer a 30-second video that broadcasts Dorito as a spirited and exciting brand. Tailored to appeal to individuals of all ages, this video showcases Dorito's fun-loving spirit, which transcends generational boundaries.

Project Details

  • Promotion of Flavor Variety: The video highlights the diverse and irresistible range of Dorito flavors, enticing viewers to embark on a flavor adventure.
  • Captivating Crunch Texture: We emphasized the textural element of the Dorito chips, zooming in on the delightful crunch with tantalizing visuals and sound.
  • Shared Fun with Friends: The video promotes the idea of communal enjoyment, encouraging people to share the joy of Dorito's crispy goodness with friends and family.
