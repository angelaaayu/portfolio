WiseBites

UX-UI Design

WiseBites is a transformative project designed to improve the quality of life for elderly individuals who face challenges related to cooking, grocery shopping, and accessing health information. Our primary focus is on enhancing the well-being of the elderly community by offering a range of essential functions and services.

Technologies Used: Figma

Objective

At WiseBites, our primary goal is to help elderly individuals live longer, healthier lives and enjoy their independence. We are dedicated to making daily life easier and more enjoyable for those who have difficulties cooking and shopping due to physical illness or disability.
We understand the unique challenges faced by elderly people, and our services are designed to address these challenges with compassion and efficiency.

Project Details

  • Health Meal Subscription Plan: We provide a comprehensive health meal subscription plan tailored to the dietary needs of our elderly customers. This subscription includes door-to-door food delivery, ensuring that nutritious meals are readily available.
  • Grocery Deal & Door-to-Door Delivery: In addition to meal subscriptions, we offer convenient grocery deals and door-to-door grocery delivery services, making it easier for our elderly clients to access essential food items.
  • Health Tips: Our platform is a valuable source of health-related information and tips, specifically curated for the elderly. We aim to empower them with knowledge that enhances their well-being.
