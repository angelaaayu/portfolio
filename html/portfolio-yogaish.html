YOGAISH

Motion Graphic

Yogaish is more than just a yoga clothing and accessories brand; it's a holistic experience that transcends the physical and embraces the spiritual. The primary focus is on women who aspire to attain mindfulness and spirituality through yoga, and we're dedicated to leading our customers on a journey that goes beyond fashion.

Technologies Used: Adobe After Effect | Adobe Illustation

Objective

Craft a visually captivating experience that wraps individuals in a comforting embrace, guiding them through each step of their yoga journey. I aspire to infuse every frame with a sense of warmth and encouragement, ensuring that viewers feel supported on their path to spiritual and physical well-being.

Project Details

I wanted to create a captivating and memorable experience that reflects the essence of the brand. The logo reveal concept is centered around the idea of using different yoga poses to interact with the logo.
This concept is symbolic of the brand's mission - to combine the physical and the spiritual. It represents the idea that through yoga, you can transform your body, mind, and spirit. The logo reveal is a visual representation of the transformative power of yoga and the possibilities it offers.
