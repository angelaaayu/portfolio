MyHumber Student Portal

UX-UI Design

MyHumber is an extensive UX/UI design project aimed at revitalizing the student portal for Humber students. The objective is to enhance user-friendliness, streamline the registration process, and foster increased engagement among students, ultimately creating a more intuitive and inviting portal experience.

Application Used: Figma

Objective

The primary objective of MyHumber is to optimize the student portal by understanding user needs and conducting thorough research on registration processes and commonly used functions. Through ideation and sketching, the project aims to redesign the portal's layout, emphasizing user-centric design principles. The UI guide and prototype phase involve creating a cohesive visual language, obtaining feedback, and refining the design to ensure a seamless and efficient user experience.

Project Details

The project begins by delving into audience needs and researching the intricacies of the registration process, identifying key functions students frequently use. Ideation and sketching follow, exploring innovative ways to present functions and identifying elements that can be streamlined. The UI guide and prototype phase involve creating a comprehensive guide for visual consistency and crafting a prototype that undergoes peer feedback and iterative revisions.
